Memoir of My Childhood

Role:

Graphic Designer & Author

Timeline:

February 2024

I created a website of an interactive poetry consisting of words, phrases, questions, and images that remind me of my childhood. The website allows the user to move every item on the website to create their own poem. The words and images are randomly placed each time the website refreshes.

With this project, I utilized my expertise in web development, graphic design, and language arts, resulting in a fusion of creativity and technical proficiency. I see these disciplines as interconnected in the way that they allow me to play with text and images.

The website can be tried at https://akalfaza.github.io/poetry-collage/
